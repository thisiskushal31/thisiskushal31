## üîÑ How to Update When Switching Companies

### Step 1: Add New Company Section
1. Go to `career-context.md` ‚Üí Work Experience section
2. Find "Company 2: [Future Company Name]" template
3. Replace `[Future Company Name]` with actual company name
4. Fill in:
   - Industry
   - Start date
   - Location
   - Company website
5. Add your first role with achievements

### Step 2: Update Previous Company
1. Find previous company section (e.g., "Company 1: Purplle.com")
2. Change "Present" to actual end date
3. Update any final achievements before leaving
4. Keep all historical data intact

### Step 3: Update Summary Sections
1. Update "Experience Summary" section:
   - Total Professional Experience
   - Companies Worked At count
   - Current Company name
   - Current Role
2. Add new company to "Experience by Company" list

### Step 4: Update LinkedIn Experience
1. Go to `career-context.md` ‚Üí LinkedIn Experience Section
2. Add new company section using Company 2 template
3. Update previous company end dates
4. Keep all historical entries

### Step 5: Maintain Historical Data
- ‚úÖ **Keep all previous companies** - Don't delete old experience
- ‚úÖ **Update dates accurately** - Use actual start/end dates
- ‚úÖ **Preserve achievements** - All historical work remains documented
- ‚úÖ **Update metrics** - Cumulative metrics across all companies
